Brief Overview of EDGE

The Economic Development Growth Engine (EDGE) is the primary economic development agency for Memphis and Shelby County. The organization brings together public and private partners to support business growth, attract investment, and expand economic opportunity across the region. EDGE administers a range of programs — including PILOT tax incentives, TIF financing, Inner City Economic Development (ICED) loans for small businesses in historically underserved neighborhoods, and industrial development bonds — all designed to strengthen local communities and encourage long‑term development. The agency also maintains a public project database to promote transparency and track outcomes.

Why I Chose This Project

I selected the EDGE public dataset because it offered the scale, complexity, and real‑world relevance I needed to grow as a data analyst and emerging data engineer. With multiple programs, thousands of records, and a mix of formats and data quality issues, EDGE provided the perfect opportunity to practice building a structured ETL pipeline from scratch. The dataset is large enough to require thoughtful engineering, yet grounded in civic impact — making it ideal for demonstrating both technical skill and the ability to extract meaningful insights from public data.

My analysis of the EDGE dataset is organized into two parts — ETL and Data Analysis/Visuals — moving from raw data to a final product that delivers clear business insights.
