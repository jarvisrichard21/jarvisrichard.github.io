Brief Overview of EDGE

The Economic Development Growth Engine (EDGE) is the primary economic development agency for Memphis and Shelby County. The organization brings together public and private partners to support business growth, attract investment, and expand economic opportunity across the region. EDGE administers a range of programs — including PILOT tax incentives, TIF financing, small business loans, and industrial development bonds — all designed to strengthen local communities and encourage long‑term development. The agency also maintains a public project database to promote transparency and track outcomes.
